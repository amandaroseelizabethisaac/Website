My Hometown

I was born and raised in Fort St. James. It is the most the beautiful place in my whole world ; I never want to leave this awesome town. There is much to do such as fishing, boating, hunting, golfing, skiing, hiking and so much more outdoors activities. I am never bored from all the things to do in and around my hometown.

Things to do in Fort St James.

Fort st James has many expriences to offer the outdoor enthusiest from far and near. It is inviting to a tourist and keeps the locals busy. The golf course hosts many tournaments through the summer while the ski hill keeps you warm throughout the winter. There are many provincial parks in Fort St. James too.

  • Murray Ridge Ski Hill
  • Camping
  • Stuart Lake Golf Course

Working in the Outdoors.

When I was 14 years old, I began working in siviculture in the St. James area. I was employed to restore and reclaim the forest. I learned the about the stages in reclamation aspect in Forstry sector. I became a Medic in 2004 and never left the field work. I have a passion for the outdoors that extended into my workforce. I was outside while I worked and when I wasn't working. I love the winters and the rain.
